What is Robotics?

Robotics is an after-school sport in which groups of high school students work together to make a robot that can compete in a game. Although the games change every year, they usually involve picking up game pieces and scoring them in a goal. In addition, games will often have other ways of scoring points, giving teams options as to what they can design their robot to do.

Walled Lake Robotics competes in two games: one in the fall and one in the spring. To learn more about our seasons, visit our OCCRA and FIRST pages.
