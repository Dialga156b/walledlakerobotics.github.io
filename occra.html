OCCRA (Oakland County Competitive Robotics Association), is a robotics program for Oakland county schools. Students build and control their robots to achive a certain goal. Each year OCCRA has a new theme and objective. OCCRA is meant to help students learn practical knowledge of mechanics, engineering, programming and physics.

Walled Lake Consolidated School District WLCSD participates in the Oakland County Competitive Robotics Association tournament known as OCCRA. The following two teams represent three high schools from Walled Lake -- Central, Northern and Western: Monsters 308 and Yeti 7178
