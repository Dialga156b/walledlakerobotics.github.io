Oakland County Competitive Robotics Association (OCCRA), is a robotics program based in Oakland County. They provide a robotics challenge every year to warm teams up for the First season. OCCRA creates a game for the teams similar to First but less complicated. To compete in these games, students are tasked to build a robot capable of playing the given game to the best of their ability. Students work as a team using their knowledge of mechanical and electrical engineering, CAD design and programming with the help of their mentors, to build and control their robots to achieve the given goal. Each year OCCRA gives a new objective for teams, like FIRST, to compete in so each year students can learn practical knowledge of mechanics, engineering, programming and physics every year in a new, fun way.

Walled Lake Consolidated School District (WLCSD) participates in OCCRA every year. Both Team 308 and Team 7178 have done well in the OCCRA competition every year. In 2022, Both Team 7178 and 308 in an alliance together got 2nd place in the competition.
