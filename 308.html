The 308 Monsters FIRST Robotics team established in 1999 is a group of high school students who participate in the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC). The FRC is an annual competition in which teams of high school students design, build, and program robots to compete in a game that combines elements of sports, engineering, and technology. The 308 Monsters team spends many hours designing, building, and testing their robot in preparation for FRC.
