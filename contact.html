Walled Lake High School Robotics team meetings for both 308 and 7178 are held on Tuesdays and Thursdays from 6-8:30pm, and on Saturdays from 9:00 am to 3:00 pm.In rooms D-117, D-118, and D-120.

If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to email Patrick Ohl at patrickohl@wlcsd.org

The mechanically curious can learn to use macinery and power drills, electronics, and circuitry. But there are other opportunities as well such as CAD Design, Software Programming, Photography, Web Design, Graphic Design, Electrial, Marketing, and more!

Students participating in Robotics gain hands on experience that is not available in the classroom. Scholarships are available to students participating in robotics for many different schools and majors.
