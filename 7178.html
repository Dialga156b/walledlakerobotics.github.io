Yetis 7178

7178, otherwise known as the Yetis, Is Walled Lake Monster's younger team. The Yetis made it to the FIRST World Championship in 2022, as well as winning the Excellence in Engineering award at the Walled Lake event and the district Championship at the state Championship. The Team has also participated in mutable events, such as the memorial day parade, school football games and pinkout events. From its cool Yeti Mascot to its even cooler team, the Walled lake Yetis are here to chill.
